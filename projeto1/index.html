The value of principles when designing.

The Intercom podcasts are great especially the series around “Intercom on Product”. This episode focuses on the value of having principles when developing a product and making decisions. It puts all stakeholders on the same page aligning with the same vision. Key when making decisions.

Des and Paul touch on Intercoms principles around “Following fundamentals”, the value of using existing design patterns, instead of reinventing the wheel. As innovation at Intercom is happening at the product level rather than the UI level. Conversely look at Tinder and Snapchat who innovated at the UI level and have made famous design patterns, with swiping cards and stories.

The value of principles when designing.

Is it realistic to expect your engineers to match your designs and get everything spot on the first time of asking? It's a really tough ask. Having close colloboration with your engineers is key to get to this goal.

This template helps you create documentation to help with alignment between your production designs and what's being developed or has been developed. The secret sauce is having good communication and putting emotions aside when comes to design alignment.
